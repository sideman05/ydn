Program Areas at a Glance

Our five areas work together to support youth leadership, economic progress, and social change

Leadership & Civic Engagement

2

Entrepreneurship & Economic Empowerment

Training, mentorship, and resources to support business growth, employment, and financial independence.

3

Education & Skills Development

Practical education, vocational training, and digital skills for success in a modern economy.

Promoting youth-inclusive policies through regional and national dialogue.

Supporting social cohesion, mental health awareness, environmental sustainability, and innovation.
