ABOUT US

Empowering Youth Across East Africa

Youth and Development Network in East Africa, founded in December 2025, is a dynamic regional youth-focused organization committed to empowering young people across the East African Community. We believe that every young person has the right and potential to shape their future and contribute meaningfully to sustainable development in their communities.

Grounded in principles of inclusion, innovation, and collaboration, our network brings together youth leaders, community groups, civil society organizations, and partners to create opportunities for growth, participation, and leadership.

Milestones

  • Dec 2025

    Youth and Development Network in East Africa was founded.

  • Identity

    Built on inclusion, innovation, collaboration, and integrity.

  • Approach

  • Today

MISSION, VISION, CORE VALUES

Mission, Vision, and Core Values

What guides our planning, delivery, and accountability across the region

Our Mission

To equip young people with the skills, knowledge, opportunities, and networks they need to transform their dreams into real achievements - economically, socially, and politically.

Our Vision

A thriving East Africa where youth are confident, skilled, and engaged as proactive agents of change in all sectors of society.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: Amplifying youth voices and prioritizing youth-led solutions.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking to solve pressing challenges.
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring access and opportunities for all young people regardless of background.
  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships with stakeholders across sectors.
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency, accountability, and respect in all actions.

Where We Work

Our activities span across the East African Community: Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, and South Sudan.

Join Us

We warmly welcome you to become part of our growing network.

Young leaders, partners, volunteers, and supporters are all welcome.

We use a participatory, youth-centered approach that places young people at the heart of decision-making, while partnering with grassroots organizations, governments, development partners, and the private sector.
